# How to Use Your Computer's Built-in Help

## 🆘 Finding Answers

Most programs have built-in manuals or links to support sites.

### Where to Look

- **Help Menus:** Look for a "Help" tab at the top of the screen.
- **Search Bars:** (e.g., Microsoft Office "Tell me what you want to do").
- **F1 Key:** Often opens the help menu in Windows programs.

### Features

Help files usually have a Table of Contents, FAQs, and a Searchable Database. If built-in help fails, search **Google** for your specific problem.
