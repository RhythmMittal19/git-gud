# Learning a New Program

## 🎓 You Know More Than You Think

New programs can be scary, but they often share similar features with programs you already know.

### Tips for Success

1.  **Look for similarities:** Most programs have `File` and `Edit` menus in the same place.
2.  **Shortcuts:** `Ctrl+C` (Copy) and `Ctrl+S` (Save) work in almost every program.
3.  **Hidden Toolbars:** If you can't find a tool, check the `View` or `Window` menu.
4.  **Google it:** Search for tutorials or YouTube videos on the specific software.
