# Bringing Your Files with You

## 📂 Portability Options

### 1. USB Flash Drives

Small, removable hard drives that plug into USB ports.

- **To Use:** Plug in -> Open Folder -> Drag and drop files.
- **Ejecting:** Right-click the drive and select **Eject/Disconnect** before pulling it out to prevent data corruption.

### 2. Cloud Storage

Services like Google Drive, OneDrive, and Dropbox.

- **Pros:** Files connect via account, not physical device. Harder to lose.
- **Cons:** Requires strong passwords and internet access.
