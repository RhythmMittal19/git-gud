# Protecting Your Computer

## 🛡️ Malware & Backups

Your computer faces threats from viruses and hard drive failure.

> **[Video: Watch the video below to learn how to protect your computer.]**

### Malware (Malicious Software)

Includes viruses, worms, and spyware.

- **Solution:** Install **Antivirus Software** (Bitdefender, Norton, Kaspersky).
- **Smart Browsing:** Do not download suspicious email attachments.

### Backing Up Data

If your computer dies, your files are gone unless backed up.

1.  **External Hard Drive:** Copy files to a physical USB drive kept in a safe place.
2.  **Online Backup:** Use services (Carbonite, Mozy, Box) to save files to the cloud automatically.

### Maintenance

- **Disk Cleanup:** Deletes temporary/junk files.
- **Disk Defragmenter:** Rearranges files on a PC hard drive to make it faster.
