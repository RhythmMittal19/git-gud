# Keeping Your Computer Clean

## 🧹 Physical Cleaning

Dust and overheating can destroy a computer.

> **[Video: Watch the video below to learn how to keep a computer clean.]**

### 1. Keyboard

- Shake upside down to remove crumbs.
- Use **Compressed Air** between keys.
- Clean keys with a cloth moistened with **rubbing alcohol**.

### 2. Mouse

- **Optical:** Wipe bottom; remove dust from light emitter.
- **Mechanical:** Remove tracking ball and clean internal rollers.

### 3. Monitor

- **WARNING:** Do NOT use glass cleaner (Windex). It damages anti-glare coatings.
- **Method:** Use a soft cloth moistened with water. Wipe gently.

### 4. Airflow

Keep the computer case fans clear of dust. Do not block air vents with books or walls.
