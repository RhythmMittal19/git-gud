# Creating a Safe Workspace

## 🪑 Computer Ergonomics

Ergonomics is the science of equipment design to reduce injury and strain.

> **[Video: Watch the video below to learn about arranging your workspace.]**

### Tips to Avoid Injury

- **Chair:** Adjust to support lower back.
- **Keyboard:** Keep wrists straight and relaxed. Consider an ergonomic keyboard.
- **Monitor:** Place 20-40 inches away, at eye level or slightly lower.
- **Breaks:** Look away from the screen often to prevent eye strain. Stand up and walk around.
- **Clutter:** Keep the desk clear to avoid awkward reaching.
