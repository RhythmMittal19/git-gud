# Basic Troubleshooting Techniques

## 🔧 How to Fix Common Problems

Troubleshooting is a process of **trial and error** and **elimination**.

### General Steps

1.  **Write down steps:** Keep track of what you tried.
2.  **Check Cables:** Is everything plugged in?
3.  **Restart:** This solves many basic software glitches.

### Specific Solutions

- **Power won't start:** Check power cord, outlet, and surge protector switch.
- **Program runs slowly:** Close and reopen it. Check for updates.
- **Program Frozen:**
  - **PC:** Press `Ctrl+Alt+Delete` > Task Manager > End Task.
  - **Mac:** Press `Command+Option+Esc` > Force Quit.
- **No Sound:** Check volume button, player controls, and ensure speakers are in the green port.
- **Screen Blank:** Check if monitor is plugged in and computer is not in Sleep mode.

**Hard Problems:** If reformatting or reinstalling the OS is required, consult a professional.
