# What is a Computer?

## 💻 Definition

A computer is an electronic device that manipulates information, or data. It has the ability to **store**, **retrieve**, and **process** data. You likely use a computer to type documents, send email, and browse the Web, but they are also used for spreadsheets, presentations, and video editing.

## Hardware vs. Software

Every computer relies on two things:

### 1. Hardware

Hardware is any part of your computer that has a **physical structure**.

- **Examples:** Keyboard, mouse, internal parts.
  ![Image: Hardware](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_hardware.jpg)

### 2. Software

Software is any **set of instructions** that tells the hardware what to do.

- **Examples:** Web browsers, games, word processors.
  ![Image: Software](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_software.jpg)

## Types of Computers

![Image: Different Types of Computer](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_types_of_computers.jpg)

Computers come in many shapes and sizes:

1.  **Desktop Computers:** Designed for desks; usually have a case, monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Used at work, home, and school.

![Image: Desktop Computer](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_desktop_computers.jpg)

2.  **Laptop Computers:** Battery-powered and portable.

![Image: Laptop Computer](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_laptop_computers.jpg)

3.  **Tablet Computers:** Handheld; uses a touch-sensitive screen for navigation (e.g., iPad).

![Image: Tablet Computer](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_tablet_computers.jpg)

4.  **Servers:** A computer that "serves" information to other computers on a network (like the internet).

![Image: Server Computer](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_servers.jpg)

**Specialized Computers:**

- **Smartphones:** Cell phones that browse the internet.
- **Wearables:** Fitness trackers and smartwatches.
- **Game Consoles:** Specialized for video games on TV.
- **TVs:** "Smart" TVs that stream internet video.

### PC vs. Mac

- **PC:** Based on the original IBM PC (1981). Typically runs Microsoft Windows.

![Image: PC](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_pc.jpg)

- **Mac:** Introduced in 1984 by Apple. Runs macOS. First widely sold computer with a Graphical User Interface (GUI).

![Image: Mac](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e0730c7dd48174331f5164_01_17_2014/whatisacomputer_mac.jpg)
