# About This Tutorial

## 👋 What is this course?

Welcome to the Computer Basics tutorial. Whether you are completely new to computers or just want to brush up on your skills, this guide will help you feel more comfortable with technology.

### What You Will Learn

We move beyond just typing and clicking. This tutorial covers:

- **Fundamental Concepts:** Understanding hardware, software, and how they work together.
- **Setup:** How to physically set up a computer and connect to the internet.
- **Maintenance:** How to keep your computer healthy, clean, and fast.
- **Safety:** Protecting your privacy and physical workspace.
