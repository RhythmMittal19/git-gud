# Understanding the Cloud

## ☁️ What is the Cloud?

"The Cloud" is the Internet. It means storing data on **internet servers** instead of your computer's hard drive.

> **[Video: Watch the video below to learn more about the cloud.]**

### Why Use It?

1.  **Convenience:** Access your email (Gmail) or files (Dropbox) from _any_ computer.
2.  **Security:** If your computer breaks, your cloud data is safe.
3.  **Sharing:** Easily share photos (Flickr, iCloud) with others.

### Web Apps

Applications that run in the cloud/browser rather than being installed.

- **Google Docs:** Office suite in the browser.
- **Facebook:** Social media profile updated in real-time.
- **Pixlr:** Image editing in the browser.
