# Setting Up a Computer

## 📦 Step-by-Step Guide

Setting up a desktop is easier than it looks.

> **[Video: Watch the video below to learn how to set up a desktop computer.]**

### The Process

1.  **Unpack:** Remove plastic/tape. Place the case in a well-ventilated area to prevent overheating.
2.  **Monitor:** Connect the monitor cable (HDMI/VGA) to the back of the computer case and the monitor.
3.  **Keyboard & Mouse:**
    - **USB:** Plug into rectangular USB ports.
    - **PS/2:** (Older) Plug purple into purple port, green into green port.
4.  **Audio:** Plug speakers/headphones into the **green** port. Microphones go in the **pink** port.
5.  **Power:** Plug the power cables into the Case and Monitor, then into a **Surge Protector**.
6.  **Peripherals:** Plug in printers/scanners (many are plug-and-play).

**Note:** If you have a laptop, simply plug in the AC adapter to charge the battery and press the power button.
