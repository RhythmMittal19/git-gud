# Mobile Devices

## 📱 Handheld Computing

Mobile devices are computers small enough to hold in your hand.

### Tablet Computers

- **Input:** Uses a touch-sensitive screen (virtual keyboard) instead of a mouse/keyboard.
- **Use Case:** Ideal as a second computer for entertainment (iPad, Android tablets).

### E-Readers

- **Purpose:** Designed mainly for reading e-books.
- **Display:** Uses **E-ink**, which is readable in bright sunlight and reduces eye strain.
- **Examples:** Amazon Kindle, Nook.

### Smartphones

- **Definition:** A powerful cell phone that connects to the internet via Wi-Fi or cellular data.
- **Features:** High-quality digital cameras, music players, GPS, and web browsing.
- **Input:** Touch-sensitive screen.
