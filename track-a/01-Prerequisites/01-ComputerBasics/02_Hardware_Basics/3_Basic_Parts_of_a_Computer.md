# Basic Parts of a Computer

## 🖥️ The Desktop Setup

The basic parts of a desktop computer are the computer case, monitor, keyboard, mouse, and power cord.

[![Video: Basic Parts of Computer](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/mLgTnkw558w/maxresdefault.jpg)](https://youtu.be/mLgTnkw558w)

### 1. Computer Case

![Image: Computer Case](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e073137dd48174331f5166_01_17_2014/basic_computer_parts_cases.jpg)

The metal and plastic box that contains the main components (Motherboard, CPU, Power Supply).

- **Desktop Case:** Lies flat on a desk (monitor sits on top).
- **Tower Case:** Tall and sits next to the monitor or on the floor.
- **All-in-One:** Internal components are built into the monitor (no separate case).

### 2. Monitor

![Image: Monitor](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e073137dd48174331f5166_01_17_2014/basic_computer_parts_monitor.jpg)

Displays images and text on the screen using a video card.

- **LCD/LED:** Newer, flat-panel displays. Thin and light.
- **CRT:** Older, heavy, boxy monitors (Cathode Ray Tube).

### 3. Keyboard

![Image: Keyboard](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e073137dd48174331f5166_01_17_2014/Windows_Keyboard_basicparts2.jpg)

The main way to communicate with a computer.

### 4. Mouse

![Image: Mouse](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e073137dd48174331f5166_01_17_2014/basic_computer_parts_mouse.jpg)

A pointing device that lets you click, move, and drag objects.

- **Optical:** Uses an electronic eye; easier to clean.
- **Mechanical:** Uses a rolling ball; requires cleaning.

**Mouse Alternatives:**

![Image: Trackpad](https://media.gcflearnfree.org/content/55e073137dd48174331f5166_01_17_2014/basic_computer_parts_touchpad.jpg)

- **Trackball:** A ball you rotate with your thumb.
- **Touchpad (Trackpad):** A touch-sensitive pad (common on laptops) where you draw with your finger.
