# Laptop Computers

## 🎒 Portability

A laptop is a personal computer designed for mobile use. It has an "All-in-one" design with a built-in monitor, keyboard, touchpad, and speakers.

> **[Video: Watch the video below to learn about laptop computers.]**

### Key Differences vs. Desktops

1.  **Touchpad:** Replaces the mouse. You drag your finger to move the cursor.
2.  **Battery:** Allows usage when not plugged in. Recharges via AC adapter. Also acts as a backup during power outages.
3.  **AC Adapter:** A specialized power cable specific to that laptop model.
4.  **Ports:** Laptops have fewer ports to save space.
5.  **Price:** Generally more expensive than desktops for the same amount of computing power.
