World's Best Lasagna

Description

This is a classic meat lasagna recipe that is cheesy, saucy, and absolutely delicious. Perfect for Sunday dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and drain fat.
  3. Mix beef with marinara sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes covered with foil.
  6. Remove foil and bake for 10 more minutes until cheese is bubbly.
