```markdown
### Metrics

- DSA problems solved: \_\_\_
- Projects completed: \_\_\_
- Applications sent: \_\_\_
- Interviews: \_\_\_
- Typing speed: \_\_\_ WPM

### Challenges

- What was hardest this month?
- What am I still struggling with?
- What took longer than expected?

### Next Month Focus

- Top 3 priorities
- Skills to develop
- Projects to complete

### Adjustments

- What's working well?
- What needs to change?
- Schedule adjustments needed?
```
