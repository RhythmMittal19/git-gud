## Schedule & Time Management

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **SUSTAINABLE SCHEDULE**
> The original 86 hrs/week is a recipe for burnout. This revised schedule is ~60-65 hrs/week — still aggressive but sustainable for 6 months.

> [!NOTE]
> Research shows deep work capacity maxes out at 4-6 hours/day. After that, retention drops significantly. Quality > Quantity.

### Daily Schedule (Weekdays) — Phase 1-3

```text
06:30 – 07:00  Wake up + Plan day + Review yesterday's notes
07:00 – 09:00  Track A — Deep Work Block 1 (2 hrs)
09:00 – 09:15  Break — walk, water, sunlight
09:15 – 11:15  Track A — Deep Work Block 2 (2 hrs)
11:15 – 11:30  Break — snack, rest eyes
11:30 – 13:00  Track A — Block 3 (1.5 hrs)
               → Total Track A morning = 5.5 hrs

13:00 – 14:00  Lunch + Full rest (NO SCREENS — this is important)

14:00 – 14:30  Job Applications (Month 4+) OR Foundation (Month 1-3)
14:30 – 16:00  Track A — Afternoon session (1.5 hrs)
               → Total Track A = 7 hrs/day

16:00 – 16:45  Foundation (typing + terminal + git) — 45 min
16:45 – 17:45  DSA — 1-2 problems (1 hr)
               → Start DSA from Week 1, not Month 3

17:45 – 18:00  Get ready → leave for badminton
18:00 – 21:00  Badminton + travel + shower + dinner (SACRED TIME)

21:00 – 22:30  Evening Session (1.5 hrs)
               → Alternating: Project work / DSA / Learning

22:30 – 23:00  Wind down, review day, plan tomorrow (no screens)
23:00 – 06:30  Sleep (7.5 hours) — NON-NEGOTIABLE
```

### Burnout Prevention Rules

> [!CAUTION]
> Burnout isn't a badge of honor. It's a productivity killer that sets you back weeks.

**Non-Negotiables:**

1. **Sleep 7+ hours** — Your brain consolidates learning during sleep
2. **Badminton/Exercise** — Keeps cognitive function high
3. **One full evening off** — Sunday evening is sacred
4. **One full day off per month** — No code, no thinking about code
5. **Lunch without screens** — Eyes and brain need rest

**Warning Signs:**

- Can't focus for more than 20 minutes
- Dreading the work you used to enjoy
- Mistakes increasing, not decreasing
- Trouble sleeping despite being tired
- Irritability, feeling hopeless

**If Burned Out:**

1. Take 2-3 full days off (no code)
2. Sleep as much as you need
3. Exercise, go outside
4. Return at 50% intensity
5. Gradually ramp back up
